About EIFS Specialties, Inc.

We are a company built on commitment, quality, and craftsmanship, and have been a trusted leader in the construction industry since 1998.

Our Values

We value our customers and their satisfaction is our primary goal. We pride ourselves on the safety and expertise of our employees who meet OSHA requirements and stay on the cutting edge of industry practices.

Our Expertise

Our employees are well trained and hardworking, taking pride in the work they do. We remain committed to quality and customer satisfaction in all our projects.

Our Partners

We work closely with leading manufacturers in the industry such as BASF Senergy CI, EIFS and Stucco Wall Systems and STO Corporation EIFS and Stucco Wall Systems.
